Our Works

Visible Light Communication

The VLC received attention as a potential means to reduce the spectral load on radio cell systems.

Our laboratory researches and experiments with various types of VLC schemes.

Machine Learning
Deep Learning

Among the various technological elements of AI, ML(Machine Learning) and DL(Deep Learning) have been attracting attention in recent years.

Our laboratory researches various machine learning and deep learning based systems that can be used in new industries such as next-generation communication and smart factories.

Internet of Things
Edge Computing

Internet of Things(IoT) is one of the most important factors in the field of the 4th industrial revolution.

Our laboratory researches various types of IoT prototypes from daily life to industrial sites.
